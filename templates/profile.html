Ice-Cream and marshmallow smoothie
Considered as a treat

How to make the smoothie:

On a foil-lined baking sheet, toast marshmallows under a broiler, turning as necessary, until all sides are golden brown. Just as the marshmallows are finishing up, put ice cream and milk in the blender. Pour shake into a glass and top with some whipped cream and some crushed graham crackers.
